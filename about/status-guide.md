## The Philosophy Behind This System

This status system reflects my belief that **knowledge development is iterative and collaborative**. By showing where each piece of content sits in my thinking process, I'm inviting you into the laboratory rather than just presenting finished products.

### Why This Matters

1. **Transparency**: You can assess how much weight to give any particular post based on its maturity
2. **Process Over Product**: Learning happens in the journey, not just the destination
3. **Collaboration**: Early-stage posts invite dialogue that improves the final work
4. **Honesty**: I don't pretend to have all the answersâ€”I'm figuring things out alongside you

### Posts Without Status Labels

Posts without any status label are considered **published and stable**. They represent my current best understanding on a topic, though I remain open to revising them if new evidence or insights emerge.
