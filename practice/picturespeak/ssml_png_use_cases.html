What is SSML-in-PNG?

Embedding Speech Synthesis Markup Language (SSML) in PNG images creates self-contained, accessible multimedia files that combine visual and audio information. This innovative approach opens up countless possibilities across education, accessibility, entertainment, and professional applications. Each image becomes a rich, interactive experience that can speak for itself.
