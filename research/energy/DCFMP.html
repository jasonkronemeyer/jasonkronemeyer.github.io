DC Microgrids for Resilient Schools

Centralized Power • Safer Networks • Lower Costs
How fault‑managed power + distributed edge architecture simplify school infrastructure and improve resiliency.

  • Fault‑managed power isolates faults instantly, keeping systems running safely.
  • Distributed edge design removes most intermediate closets and AC power points.
  • Cleaner, safer power distribution improves reliability across all school wings.

How It Works

DC Microgrid Introduced
High‑voltage DC enables centralized, campus‑wide distribution.
Fault‑Managed Power
Class 4 DC actively monitors and shuts off faults within milliseconds.
Distributed Edge Network
Devices like ONTs and switches are powered centrally via hybrid fiber‑copper.
Unified Backup Power
A single battery or generator protects every wing of the school.

Practical Outcome

Schools gain lower operating costs, better outage protection, and safer classrooms by replacing scattered backup batteries with one unified, resilient power architecture.
